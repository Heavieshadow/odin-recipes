Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

This is what you'll need.

  • Chicken Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

How to make scrambled eggs

Step by step guide to the finished product.

  • Whisk the eggs, salt and pepper.
  • Heat the cooking oil in a non stick pan over medium heat.
  • Add egg mixture, wait 5 seconds then start leisurely running a rubber spatula back and forth across the pan.
  • After a minute (60 seconds) start folding the eggs over and repeated the process until raw runny egg no longer spreads out onto the pan and you have a pile of soft done egg.
  • Remove from the stove, stir until cooked to your taste. You can return it to the stove if you want your egg cooked more.
